How to:

  • Press the hotkey selected above and the left mouse button, then draw the rectangle with your mouse! Click on the rectangle to make it disappear!
  • If secondary background color is checked, a rectangle drawn above another rectangle will get a different background color.
  • If random background color is checked, the primary and secondary background colors will be ignored, and each rectangle will get a random generated background color from a predefined list.
